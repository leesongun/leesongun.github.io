Simple Proof of the Butterfly Theorem

Jan 18, 2020

I “discovered” a simple proof of the butterfly theorem in classical geometry in 2012 or so.

Statement

Let M be the midpoint of a chord PQ of a conic, through which two other chords AB and CD are drawn; AD and BC intersect chord PQ at X and Y correspondingly. Then M is the midpoint of XY. 1
